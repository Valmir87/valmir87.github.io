Mini Cooper — Maintenance

Repair & Restoration
Front Suspension Replacement
Clean InstallRoad Validated
Why

Right front strut seal failed. Went DIY over the dealer to learn the system and hand-pick the components.

Process

Pulled the wheel, disconnected the brake line and ABS, unbolted the strut, and safely swapped in a new Bilstein with the OEM spring.

Result

Validated on a Lethbridge → Whistler road trip. Cleaner damping through highways and passes, no vibrations, no instability.

Interior Repair
Door Handle Disassembly
Clean RepairNo Parts Used
Why

Interior handle stopped working. Wanted to diagnose the root cause before throwing parts at it.

Process

Pulled the door trim cleanly, disassembled the handle mechanism, and traced the failure to a misrouted cable and failed clip seat.

Result

Handle restored to full function — no new parts, no broken clips. Fixed through methodical disassembly and diagnosis.
